Yes. Any operating system with a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) works. It's been tested on all three.

Your data never leaves your device. Everything runs locally in your browser. No server, no upload, no cloud storage of any kind.

Yes. You can use any Forgekit tool for personal and commercial work — client reports, business documents, anything. One licence per person.
